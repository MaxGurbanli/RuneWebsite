Update v1.10

On December 18th, 2021


With winter exams over, Rune wastes no time to bring new features as soon as possible.
This is a big general update introducing a new Moderator feature and Economy changes.

Auto-role: It's finally here!
Sharpen up your servers with an easily configurable new !!settings autorole [add/remove/list] command.

See your current auto-role list with !!settings autorole list


Add a new role to the list with !!settings autorole add [@Role/ID]


Similarly, removing roles works using !!settings autorole remove [@Role/ID]


In addition, if an existing role is deleted, it is automatically removed from the auto-role!

Search is getting a revamp!
The moment you have all been waiting for, the !!search command now uses buttons instead of messages!


Other Economy changes
- Maximum amount of coins you can bet was increased 750 -> 1000
- The reward for guessing correctly on !!numberguesser on your 6th (last) attempt tried giving you 0.5 coins if you had no bet. Rune stopped using decimals a while ago, so this value has been increased 0.5 -> 1

General bug fixes
- The !!compare returned an error when trying to compare yourself with someone who has never used Rune before. This is now fixed. Oops!
- The !!balance command got confused and showed your inventory worth when used on another member. This is also fixed. Yuck!
- Member profile update logs weren't sent to your log channel! They are now.

And probably many other minor improvements and bug fixes that I forgot to mention! Lastly, a lot of code for commands such as blackjack, buy, sell, etc. was also optimized to make it easier to build on top of in the future.

That's all for now! A lot of this comes from your feedback - feel free to share your thoughts in the boxes below.
