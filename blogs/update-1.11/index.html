Update v1.11

On December 22nd, 2021


This is a moderator-focused update implementing Discord's very recently released timeout system.

Mute: show your server members who's boss
- Added !!mute [@Member/ID] [Duration] command
--> To timeout a member
- Added !!unmute [@Member/ID] command
--> To unmute a timed out member
- Added !!mutes command
--> To view all the currently timed out members





The mute command has arrived! Take advantage of Discord's new Member Timeout feature to prevent members from chat or react in text channels, and connect to voice or Stage channels.

The duration option in the !!mute command uses smart time parsing to process the timeout duration. Accepted values are s for seconds, m for minutes, h for hours, and d for days. For example, to mute a member for 12 hours, use !!mute @Member 12h. If you want to mute them for 10 minutes, use !!mute @member 10m.

Timeouts can last up to (not including) 28 days. This means the longest possible timeout time is 671 hours, or just under 28 days. Technically you can go even closer by converting into minutes or seconds.

Members in timeout receive the following effects:
-> Disables posting or reacting to messages in text chat
-> Prevents them from connecting to voice or stage channels
-> Automatically disconnects them from a voice or stage channel, if they are in one

When using mute/unmute commands, special moderation logs are sent to your server's log channels!


More bug fixes
- Fixed logging related commands not working
- Fixed !!balance bank level progress bars not being displayed on mobile

That's all for now! A lot of this comes from your feedback - feel free to share your thoughts in our Discord server.
