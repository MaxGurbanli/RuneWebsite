Commands

List of bot commands

dadjoke
Retrieves a randomly generated dad joke.
!!dadjoke
serveravatar
Grab the current server's avatar icon.
!!serveravatar
blackjack
Play a game of blackjack to earn coins.
!!blackjack [bet or "none"]
addbot
Invite Rune to your server.
!!addbot
coins
Shows the amount of coins a user has in their account.
!!coins (@Member/ID)
shop
Displays the coins shop.
!!shop
withdraw
Withdraw coins from your bank to your wallet.
!!withdraw [number/all]
meme
Sends a button which you can continously press to receive memes.
!!meme
deposit
Deposit your coins from your wallet to the bank for safe-keeping.
!!deposit [number/all]
grenade
Use a grenade item and potentially explode another user, stealing their precious coins.
!!grenade [@Member/ID]
advice
Randomly generated advice using adviceslip.com API.
!!advice
leaderboard
See the coins leaderboard.
!!coinslb
credits
Shows information on the developers of Rune.
!!credits
settings
See the Rune customizations you have set for this server.
!!settings
clean
Clean up Rune's messages in this channel.
!!clean (number - default 10)
whois
Shows details about you or a specified Discord account.
!!whois (@Member/ID)
work
Working can be very tiring - not with Rune though! Work every 4 hours to earn coins.
!!work
inventory
View your purchased items/perks inventory.
!!inventory
stealemoji
Stealing is bad, but there are exceptions. Nitro users can add emojis from other servers with this command.
!!stealemoji [emoji from another server]
avatar
Get a full sized image/gif avatar of a discord account.
!!avatar (@User/ID)
upgrade
Upgrade your bank storage! Store more coins by increasing your maximum capacity.
!!upgrade
slowmode
Set or remove the current channel's slowmode.
!!slowmode [time or 0]
use
Use an item from your inventory.
!!use (item)
hug
Use this to show your appreciation to a member by hugging them.
!!hug [@Member/ID] (reason)
prefix
Customize Rune's prefix for this server.
!!prefix (new prefix)
numberguesser
Guess the number to earn coins.
!!guesser [bet or "none"]
logchannel
Set or remove Rune's logging channel.
!!logchannel (#channel/clear)
logmembers
Set whether Rune should log member joins and leaves in your server's logchannel.
!!logmembers (enable/disable)
crime
Crime is fun, as long as it is legal! Commit a crime - high risk, high reward to earn coins.
!!crime
tictactoe
Play a game of tic-tac-toe against a friend!
!!tictactoe [@Member/ID]
logdeletes
Set whether Rune should log message deletions in your server's logchannel.
!!logdeletes (enable/disable)
dig
Dig with a shovel! A chance to earn coins.
!!dig
logprofiles
Set whether Rune should profile updates in your server's logchannel, this applies to username, avatar, and discriminator changes.
!!logprofiles (enable/disable)
hunt
Go hunting for animals! A chance to earn coins.
!!hunt
ignorechannel
Manage which channels Rune's logs should ignore.
!!ignorechannel [#channel/ID]
fish
Go fishing! A chance to earn coins.
!!fish
role
Add 1 or multiple roles at once to a member.
!!role [@Member/ID] [Roles]
search
Search a place for some coins, maybe you'll find some.
!!search
beg
Do a little begging, maybe someone will be kind enough to donate a few coins.
!!beg
gift
Gift your coins to another member. There is a 4% tax on transfers.
!!gift [@Member/ID] [number of coins]
heist
Start a bank robbery and push through minigames and hard choices to go big or go home, potentially earning a lot of coins. This can be done once a day.
!!heist
sell
Sell an item that you have in your inventory.
!!sell [item ID]
sync
Synchronize permissions of the current channel to the category it is in.
!!sync
ping
Returns the bot's latency (response time), in miliseconds.
!!ping
help
Shows the help selection menu, command categories, or information on a specific command.
!!help (command)
say
Make the bot say a message.
!!say [channel] [message]
devserver
An invite to Rune's support server.
!!devserver
vote
Create a vote in a specified channel with automatically added yes/no reactions.
!!vote [channel] [topic]
